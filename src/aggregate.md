# Aggregation and Subqueries

Within this chapter, we will explore aggregations and subqueries. These mechanisms are used to collect and calculate data on a subset of data and associate them with a subset of the entry.

Aggregate functions and `GROUP BY` will explored along with subqueries as they are typically used in conjuction. The following topics will be covered within this chapter.

* Aggregates and Group By
* Applying Predicates on aggregates
* Subqueries
* Distinct and Correlated Subqueries
